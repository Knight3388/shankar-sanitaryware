About Us

Your One-Stop Home Building Store in Shahjahanpur

Shankar Sanitaryware is a complete home-materials store serving Shahjahanpur and the wider Alwar region. We source tiles directly from Morbi (Gujarat) — India's tile capital — and marble from Kishangarh (Rajasthan), Asia's biggest marble market. We stock Hindware sanitaryware, hold an authorised Jaquar dealership, and are proud selling partners of Gravity. Along with retail, we also supply to other sellers and dealers at wholesale rates.
